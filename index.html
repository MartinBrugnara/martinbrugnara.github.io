About

I received my PhD in Computer Science University of Trento, Italy. There, I have been Teaching Assistant for the Database Systems course [16-21] and I am member of the Data Management Group (dbTrento).

I love experimenting with cutting edge technologies and programming languages. When not hacking on my *nix machines or wandering in space-related sciences, from physics to orbital mechanics, I enjoy skiing and swimming.

Research Interest

I am interested in database, data mining and graphs.

I worked on distributed graph processing, graph management, and data exploration, in particular, for the democratization of data repositories.
